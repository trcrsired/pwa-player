Privacy Policy

Last updated:

This Privacy Policy explains how PWA Player (“we”, “our”, or “us”) handles information when you use our application (the “App”).

1. No data collection

PWA Player does not collect, store, transmit, or process any personal data or usage data. The App runs entirely on your device and does not send any information to external servers.

2. Local functionality only

All features of the App operate locally within your browser or device. Any media you load, settings you adjust, or files you interact with remain on your device and are never transmitted to us.

3. No third‑party analytics or tracking

The App does not use analytics services, tracking scripts, advertising networks, or third‑party data collectors of any kind.

4. Permissions

If the App requests access to local files or device features, this access is used solely to provide the functionality you request (such as playing media). All permissions remain on your device and are fully controlled by you.
